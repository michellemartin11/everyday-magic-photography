About Me

Hi! I’m Michelle, a lifestyle photographer based in Fuquay-Varina and proud mom to three beautiful daughters. Photography has been my passion for as long as I can remember. Growing up, I dreamed of creating memories through the lens, and today, I’m lucky to spend my days capturing the magic that lives in the everyday moments of family life.

I strive to create a relaxed and fun experience where families can be themselves, allowing me to capture authentic, natural moments that reflect the love and joy in each family. Whether it’s family portraits, candid shots of little ones, or special celebrations, I’m here to capture the beauty of your story. I believe that the most cherished photos come from genuine connections, and I’m honored to help you preserve those precious memories.

I understand how exciting it is to see your photos as soon as possible! I aim to deliver your fully edited gallery within two weeks, but I often complete it in just a few days. My goal is to ensure every image reflects the joy and authenticity of your session, so each photo is carefully selected and thoughtfully edited.
